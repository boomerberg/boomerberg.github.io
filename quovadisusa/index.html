Quo vadis, USA?

Current Macroeconomic Snapshot of the United States. Inspired by Rüdiger Bachmann’s slides Slides (PDF)

Average Joe

How is the economy doing for most Americans? Yes, the U.S. experienced solid real GDP growth after the Covid crisis (light blue line). Much, much better than Europe and, of course, Germany. But real wages have only been rising again since 2022 – and very slowly. Labor force participation (dark blue line) has never recovered to its pre-crisis level, and the share of labor income in total income has declined throughout the Biden era.
