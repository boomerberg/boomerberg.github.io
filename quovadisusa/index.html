Quo Vadis USA?

Current Macroeconomic Analysis/ Snapshot of the United States

Average Joe

How is the economy doing for most Americans? Yes, the U.S. experienced solid real GDP growth after the Covid crisis (light blue line). Much, much better than Europe and, of course, Germany. But real wages have only been rising again since 2022 – and very slowly. Labor force participation (dark blue line) has never recovered to its pre-crisis level, and the share of labor income in total income has declined throughout the Biden era. In the end, he was not the president of Main Street. What comes next under Trump?
